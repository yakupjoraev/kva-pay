What You will get

  • Payment statuses

    Success, Unconfirmed, Payment Pending, Waiting for approval, Expired — all payment statuses are available in the API, payment form and in the merchant’s personal cabinet.

  • Fast and secure transaction

    KvakPay technologies provide fast and secure transactions compared to conventional cryptocurrency transfers. Transactions are not lost and are securely tracked in blockchain networks.

  • Wallet

    Sign up and get a free cryptocurrency wallet. Send and receive payments, deposit and withdraw. Store and exchange your cryptocurrency.

  • Volatility protection

    The automatic conversion feature allows you to convert all incoming payments to your preferred currency. For example, you can set up conversion of incoming BTC payments to EURO. It’s completely free of charge!

  • Customize network fees

    You can define the percentage of fee coverage between you and the customer for each payment yourself

  • Detailed reporting

    View and export the history of all transactions on your account. Find the transactions you need with easy-to-use filters. Track the status of crypto transactions online. Keep your business expenses under control!
