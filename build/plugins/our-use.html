  • Auto-Convert

    The cryptocurrency auto-convert function allows you to convert all incoming payments into cryptocurrencies or fiat currencies convenient for you. For example, you can set up conversion of incoming payments from BTC to EUR at the current exchange rate. It is completely free of charge!

  • Flexible currency fees

    You can give your customers a discount or additional commissions ranging from 0% to 100% on each individual coin.

  • Payments statuses

    All payment statuses — Successful, Unconfirmed, Pending Payment, Pending Approval, Overdue — are available in the API and in the merchant’s personal cabinet. You can track the status of each transaction as well.
