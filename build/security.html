Security

Learn all about the safety of your funds

  • SSL connection

    SSL encrypted connection via HTTPS, two-factor authentication, trusted IP addresses.

  • Privacy

    We store transaction information for exactly as long as it is needed to make a payment and then delete it.

  • Anonymity

    We respect the privacy of our customers and do not require proof of identity. Your payments remain anonymous.

How to improve security

Follow simple tips to improve your safety

  • 01

    Use 2-FA

    Activate the two-factor authentication option in your personal cabinet. With its help you will be able to protect your account from unauthorized access.

  • 02

    Do not store data on the Internet

    We strongly recommend storing your passwords in an offline storage, or better yet, on a physical disk. This way, you will protect your data from intruders and secure your account.

  • 03

    Use protective software

    If the device from which you use the service is not protected by antivirus, you may be at risk of phishing or other scams. Install protective software on your computer or other device to avoid such problems.
